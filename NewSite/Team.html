Kauntheya Sivananthan

Chairman & Chief Executive Officer

Having spent most of his initial working years in building and growing successful new business, it was but the obvious step for K - as he is fondly called by his friends and colleagues- to start on his own. Circa 2007 found him architecting the step up of iHorse Technologies. Responsible for formulating and executing the company's strategy, K has been at helm of all key strategic initiatives at iHorse ever since its inception. Kauntheya holds a Master in Business Administration with Computer Science Background. Prior to iHorse, he has launched three startups in the international market.

Beyond the office, Kauntheya is a passionate painter and an avid sports buff
